Bio

My interests are in all that relates to Statistics, Data Science, and Artificial Intelligence. I am currently part of the Innovation Team within PrediSurge, working on LLM projects in the medical domain. Previously, I was an AI Engineer at APTIV working on automobile projects. I also have experience as an Android developer.

Academically, I hold a Master's Degree in AI & Data Science from the Data ScienceTech Institute. Prior to that, I earned two Bachelor's Degrees. The first one was in Statistics & Data Analytics at the Seconda Università di Napoli, where I was awarded a merit scholarship to pursue a second joint one in Computer Science at the Université Sorbonne Paris Nord. I also spent a year at Tomsk State University studying Software Engineering.

Personally, I am a very curious person. I love learning about other cultures, perspectives, and intellectual domains. The variety of MOOCs I have taken across different subjects and topics can attest to that ! In the meantime, something called Formal Epistemology remains a center lifelong passion.

As for hobbies, I consider myself an amateur guitar player in the Bluegrass and Country music traditions. I am also a big fan of Andalusian and Arabic music. My top favourite singers include Earl Scruggs, Charley Pride, Johnny Cash, Édith Piaf, Omar Metioui, and Fairuz. When it is sunny outside, I often go mountain/tour biking, hiking, or kayaking !
